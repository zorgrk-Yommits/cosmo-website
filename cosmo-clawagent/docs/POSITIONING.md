# COSMO Strategic Positioning v4.0

> **Priority: HIGH — strategic realignment.** This document is the canonical
> public positioning of COSMO. It supersedes the RFQ/TRANSACT-provider framing
> of Whitepaper v3.1 and earlier. Effective 2026-06-26.

## The realignment

COSMO is **no longer positioned primarily as an RFQ / TRANSACT provider**.
COSMO is the **Execution Layer for the Agent Economy** on Supra.

| Layer | Owner | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Coordination | **SupraOS** | Agent coordination layer — how agents discover, negotiate, orchestrate |
| Markets | **SupraFX** | Market / liquidity / trading rails — price, depth, venues |
| Execution | **COSMO** | Turns autonomous intent into accountable, atomic on-chain execution |

**COSMO complements SupraFX and SupraOS. It is explicitly NOT a competitor.**

## Canonical positioning statement

> COSMO explores the execution layer for the Agent Economy on Supra. It is not a
> competitor to SupraFX. SupraOS coordinates agents, SupraFX provides market and
> liquidity rails, and COSMO turns autonomous intent into accountable execution.
